## (Body) Body

Difficulty: *Base*

Parent: *N/A*

**E - Epic Regeneration** - You regain a Body resource at the start of your turn.

**M - Hard Skin** - For whatever reason your skin becomes hard to
penetrate, reducing the price to pay when hit by 2.
